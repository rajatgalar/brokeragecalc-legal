Brokerage Calc : Upstox Zerodha  ·  By Rajat Galar

📋 Overview

This page explains how to request deletion of your data associated with Brokerage Calc : Upstox Zerodha.

✅ Good news: Most of your data (trade history, preferences, calculator values) is stored only on your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app. No action needed from us.

📦 What Data We Hold

Data Type Where Stored Action
Trade history, preferences, calculator values Your device only Deleted on uninstall
FCM device token (for notifications) Firebase (Google) Deleted on request
Purchase & subscription history RevenueCat servers Kept for 90 days after request*

* Purchase records may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and financial compliance purposes before being permanently deleted.

🔧 How to Delete Your Data

Option 1 — Delete local data instantly (no email needed):

1
Go to your device Settings → Apps → Brokerage Calc
2
Tap Storage → Clear Data — this deletes all local trade history, preferences, and calculator data immediately
3
Or simply Uninstall the app — all local data is permanently deleted

Option 2 — Request deletion of RevenueCat/Firebase data:

1
Send an email to us with subject line: "Data Deletion Request — Brokerage Calc"
2
Include your Google Play account email so we can identify your records
3
We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm via email once done
