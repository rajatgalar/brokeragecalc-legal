🔐 Privacy Policy

Brokerage Calc : Upstox Zerodha  ·  By Rajat Galar

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 1, 2026

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Brokerage Calc : Upstox Zerodha ("the App"), developed by Rajat Galar ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Short version: Your trade data never leaves your device. We don't have a server. We don't sell your data. We don't even see it.

📦 Data We Collect

1. Data you enter (Trade data)

  • Quantity, buy price, sell price, broker selection
  • Trade history entries you save manually
  • Quick Add preferences
  • App theme preference
✅ This data is stored only on your device using Android's local storage. It is never uploaded to any server, never shared, and never accessible by us.

2. Automatically collected data (via third-party SDKs)

  • Firebase (Google) — collects your device token (FCM token) to deliver push notifications. Also collects basic device info (OS version, device model) for crash reporting.
  • RevenueCat — collects purchase history and subscription status to manage your premium subscription. This is processed securely by RevenueCat on their servers.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate brokerage charges accurately on your device
  • To save your trade history and preferences locally
  • To send you relevant push notifications (new features, updates)
  • To manage your premium subscription status
  • To restore your purchase if you reinstall the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

💾 Local Storage

All trade data, preferences, and history you create in the App are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore and Room Database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by us
  • Is not accessible to us in any way

💳 Subscriptions & Billing

The App offers optional premium subscriptions (Monthly and Annual) processed through Google Play Billing and managed by RevenueCat.

  • Payments are processed by Google Play — we never see your payment details
  • RevenueCat stores your purchase receipt and subscription status securely
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled 24 hours before the renewal date
  • To cancel, go to Google Play Store → Subscriptions

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

🔔 Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications about app updates, new features, and relevant information. Notifications are sent to topic groups:

  • all_users — general announcements sent to all users
  • premium_users — premium-specific updates

You can disable notifications at any time from your device's Settings → Apps → Brokerage Calc → Notifications.

Firebase Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

🔗 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — policies.google.com/privacy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

👶 Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 18 and above as it relates to stock market trading and financial calculations. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

🛡️ Data Security

Since all your trade data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

For subscription data, RevenueCat uses industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your purchase information.

⚖️ Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings
  • Cancel your subscription at any time via Google Play
  • Request deletion of your RevenueCat data by contacting us

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
